# Thinking… indicator with elapsed timer

Some models pause to "think" before they start writing — especially the reasoning-heavy ones. Instead of leaving you wondering whether anything is happening, Kite shows a "Thinking…" indicator with a live elapsed timer.

You can see at a glance whether the model has been at it for two seconds or two minutes, which makes it easier to decide whether to wait or cancel. Once tokens start streaming, the indicator disappears and the reply takes over.
