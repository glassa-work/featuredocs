# Pin conversations

Some conversations are reference material you come back to all the time — a recipe collection, a long debugging thread, a shopping list. Pin them so they stay at the top of the drawer instead of drifting down as you start new chats.

Long-press a conversation in the drawer and pick "Pin". Pinned chats show a small pin icon and group together in their own section above the regular date-grouped list. Long-press and unpin to send one back into the normal flow.
