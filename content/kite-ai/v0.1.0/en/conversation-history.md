# Conversation history

Every conversation you have in Kite AI is saved automatically on your device. Nothing is lost when you close the app or restart your phone.

## Finding a past chat

Open the side drawer to see all your conversations, with the most recent at the top. Tap any one to jump back in — the full message history loads instantly, including any images you attached.

## Privacy

History lives locally. The only network calls Kite makes are the ones to your chosen model provider when you send a message.
