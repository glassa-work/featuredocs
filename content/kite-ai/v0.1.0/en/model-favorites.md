# Favorite a model from the picker

Open the model picker and tap the star next to any model to favorite it. Favorited models are pulled into a "Favorites" section at the top of the picker, above the full provider lists.

This is the fastest way to keep your three or four go-to models within easy reach, especially if you have many providers configured. Tap the star again to unfavorite. Combined with the searchable picker, switching models becomes a one-tap operation most of the time.
