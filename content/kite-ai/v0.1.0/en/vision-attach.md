# Attach images for vision

Many modern models can look at pictures, not just text. In Kite AI you can tap the attach button next to the composer to add one or more images from your camera or photo library, then send them along with your prompt.

The model will see the images and your message together — handy for asking about a screenshot, identifying something in a photo, transcribing handwriting, or describing a chart. Make sure the model you have selected supports vision; the picker shows which ones do.
