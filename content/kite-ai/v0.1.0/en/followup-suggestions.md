# Follow-up suggestion chips

After each reply, Kite suggests a few natural follow-up questions as tappable chips just below the message. They are written based on the model's answer, so they make sense in context.

Tap one to send it as your next message — no typing required. Suggestions are great when you want to keep exploring a topic but are not sure what to ask next, and they make Kite friendlier for first-time users who are still finding their feet.
