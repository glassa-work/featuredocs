# Multi-provider chat

Kite AI is not tied to one model vendor. Bring your own key for Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, Together, Groq, OpenRouter, or point Kite at any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint, and chat with all of them from the same place.

## Switching providers

Open the model picker at the top of the chat screen to see every model your configured providers expose. Switching is instant — your conversation history stays put, and your next message will be sent to the model you just picked.

## Adding a provider

Head to Settings, choose a provider, and paste in your API key. Custom endpoints take a base URL plus key so you can talk to self-hosted or proxied models the same way.
