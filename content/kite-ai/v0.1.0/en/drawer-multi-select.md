# Multi-select drawer with batch actions

Cleaning up dozens of old chats one at a time is tedious. The drawer now has a multi-select mode for batch actions.

## Entering select mode

Long-press any conversation in the drawer to enter select mode. The row gets a checkbox and a selection toolbar slides in at the top.

## Selecting

Tap any other conversation to add it to the selection, or tap "Select all" to grab everything in view. Tap a selected row to deselect it.

## Batch actions

From the toolbar you can delete, pin, or unpin all selected conversations in one go. Confirm the action and the drawer updates immediately. Tap "Cancel" to leave select mode without changing anything.
