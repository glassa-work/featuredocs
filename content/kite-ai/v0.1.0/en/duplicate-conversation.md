# Duplicate a conversation

Sometimes you want to branch off an existing chat without losing the original — try a different angle, ask a sensitive follow-up, or save a snapshot before continuing. Long-press a conversation in the drawer and pick "Duplicate".

Kite makes a full copy with every message, attachment, and setting. The copy lands at the top of the drawer with " (copy)" appended to the title, ready for you to rename and continue independently of the original.
