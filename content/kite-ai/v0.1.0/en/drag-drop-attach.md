# Drag-and-drop image attach (desktop)

On desktop platforms, you can now drag an image straight from your file manager or browser onto the Kite window. The composer highlights as you hover, and dropping the image attaches it to your next message.

You can drop several images at once, mix it with text you have already typed, and remove any of them before sending. It is the fastest way to start a vision conversation when you already have the picture in front of you.
