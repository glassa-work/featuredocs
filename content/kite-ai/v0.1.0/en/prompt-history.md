# Up-arrow prompt history recall

With the composer empty, press the up arrow on a hardware keyboard to recall your last prompt. Press up again to step further back through your history; press down to come forward.

It works the same way a terminal does, and it is a fast way to re-send a prompt with a small tweak — for example to ask "translate that to Spanish too" with the original message right there as a starting point. Editing the recalled text and sending starts a new entry in the history.
