# Auto-scroll lock + jump-to-latest

While a reply is streaming, Kite normally keeps the view glued to the latest token. That used to make it impossible to scroll up and re-read something earlier in the chat without being yanked back to the bottom.

Now, the moment you scroll away from the bottom, auto-scroll pauses. A small "Jump to latest" pill appears at the bottom of the screen so you can return whenever you want. New messages keep streaming in the background; you just stay where you put yourself.
