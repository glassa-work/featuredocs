# Display Mode — Dedicated Family Display

Turn any tablet or old phone into a dedicated family display. Display Mode transforms Nesta into a glanceable, always-on screen that the whole household can read from across the room.

::video[videos/demo_kiosk_mode.mp4]

## How It Works

Display Mode is designed for a device that sits on your kitchen counter, mounted on a wall, or placed on a shelf. When activated:

1. The screen stays awake (prevents auto-sleep)
2. The UI scales up for distance reading
3. Navigation is simplified — no menus, no settings
4. Content auto-rotates between today's timeline, upcoming week, and family notes

## Key Features

- **Large Typography** — event titles readable from 3 meters away
- **Auto-Brightness** — adjusts based on ambient light
- **Child-Safe** — no access to settings, app store, or other apps
- **Auto-Refresh** — updates every 60 seconds without interaction
- **Night Mode** — dims automatically after a configured bedtime

## Setting Up Display Mode

To enable Display Mode:

1. Open Nesta Settings
2. Tap "Display Mode"
3. Choose your rotation interval (30s, 60s, or 120s)
4. Tap "Start Display Mode"
5. The device will enter full-screen display

To exit, tap and hold the bottom-right corner for 3 seconds.

## Screens in Rotation

Display Mode cycles through three views:

### Today's Timeline
The standard day view, scaled up with larger fonts and higher contrast. Perfect for the morning rush.

### Week Ahead
A simplified week view showing one line per day with the most important events. Helps the family plan ahead without pulling out their phones.

### Family Notes
A shared notes board where family members can post short messages. Think of it as a digital sticky note board.

## Why Display Mode?

Most family calendar apps expect everyone to pull out their phone. But the best information display is passive — a glance should be enough. Display Mode brings Nesta closer to the experience of a physical family board, without the hassle of updating it by hand.

> "We mounted a cheap tablet on the kitchen wall. Now everyone checks it on their way out. No more 'I did not know about that.'"

## Hardware Recommendations

Display Mode works best on:

- Any tablet with a 10-inch or larger screen
- Old phones mounted in landscape orientation
- Dedicated display devices (like Amazon Fire tablets)

The key is a device you do not use for anything else. This keeps Display Mode running uninterrupted.
