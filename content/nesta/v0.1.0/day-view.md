# Day View — Your Family's Timeline

The day view is what you see when you glance at your family display. It shows everything happening today, organized by category so nothing falls through the cracks.

::video[videos/demo_day_view.mp4]

## How It Works

When you open Nesta, the day view is front and center. Events are grouped into categories:

- **School** — drop-off, pick-up, after-school activities
- **Meals** — breakfast, lunch, dinner plans
- **Activities** — sports, lessons, playdates
- **Tasks** — household chores, errands, reminders

## Key Features

- Category-grouped timeline with color-coded headers
- Past items automatically fade to 60% opacity
- Auto-scrolls to the current time on launch
- Tap any event to see full details

## Design Philosophy

We deliberately avoided a traditional calendar grid. Families do not think in 30-minute increments — they think in moments: "after school," "before dinner," "bedtime." The day view reflects that mental model.

> "It is like a paper schedule pinned to the fridge, but it updates itself."

## Tips

1. Swipe left or right to move between days
2. Pull down to refresh shared events from family members
3. Long-press any category header to collapse or expand it
